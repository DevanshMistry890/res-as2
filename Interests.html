The "Mortal Kombat" movie is action movie of the iconic video geme franchise. It gathers the famous fighters for a grand tournament where they compete to decide Earth's fate. This film offers amazing martial arts action with well-executed combat scenes, striking cinematography, and beloved characters like Scorpion and Liu Kang.

This game offers a special fusion of action, skill, and strategy with gorgeous graphics and a interesting plot.
